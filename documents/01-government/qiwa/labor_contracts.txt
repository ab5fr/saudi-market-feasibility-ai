TITLE: Qiwa Platform - Labor Contracts and Employee Management
AUTHORITY: Qiwa (Ministry of Human Resources and Social Development)
URL: https://qiwa.sa
DATE: 2024

## Overview
Qiwa is the unified platform for managing labor affairs in Saudi Arabia. It handles employment contracts, wage protection, and Saudization (Nitaqat) compliance.

## Mandatory Services

### 1. Employment Contract Management
- **Digital Contracts:** All contracts must be created through Qiwa
- **Contract Types:**
  - Fixed-term contracts
  - Indefinite contracts
  - Project-based contracts
  - Part-time contracts

### 2. Wage Protection System (Mudad)
- **Purpose:** Ensure timely salary payments
- **Requirement:** All salaries must be paid through approved banks
- **Timeline:** Within 10 days of month end
- **Penalties:** Non-compliance affects Nitaqat rating

### 3. Nitaqat (Saudization) Program
- **Green Zone:** Highest Saudization compliance
- **Red Zone:** Risk of penalties and restrictions

## Saudization Requirements by Company Size

| Employees | Minimum Saudi % |

## Employee Onboarding Process

### Step 1: Contract Creation
1. Login to https://qiwa.sa
2. Select "Create Contract"
3. Enter employee details
4. Define salary and benefits
5. Employee accepts via Absher

### Step 2: GOSI Registration
- Automatic upon contract acceptance
- Must register employee within 30 days

### 3. Mudad Registration
- Link bank account for wage payments
- Ensure timely monthly transfers

## Required Documents

For New Saudi Employees:
- Copy of ID
- Qualification certificates
- Medical insurance
- Bank account details

For Expatriate Employees:
- Valid work visa
- Medical insurance (mandatory)
- Qualification attestation
- Bank account details

## Wage Requirements (2024)

### Minimum Wages
- **Saudi Employees:** No legal minimum (market-based)
- **Expatriate Employees:** No legal minimum
- **Common Practice:** SAR 3,000-5,000 for entry level

### Benefits Requirements
- Medical insurance (mandatory for all)
- End-of-service benefits
- Annual leave (21 days for first 5 years, 30 days after)
- Sick leave (30 days full pay, 60 days half pay)

## Contract Termination

### Notice Period
- **Fixed-term contracts:** 30 days notice
- **Indefinite contracts:** 60 days notice

### End-of-Service Benefits
- **First 5 years:** Half month salary per year
- **After 5 years:** Full month salary per year

## Qiwa Platform Fees
- **Contract Creation:** Free
- **Contract Cancellation:** SAR 200
- **Employee Transfer:** SAR 200

## Integration with Other Systems

- **Absher:** Employee authentication
- **GOSI:** Social insurance
- **Mudad:** Wage protection
- **Nitaqat:** Saudization tracking

## Important Compliance Dates

- **Monthly Wages:** Must be paid by 10th of next month
- **Contract Renewals:** 30 days before expiry
- **GOSI Contributions:** 15th of next month
- **Nitaqat Updates:** Quarterly

## Penalties for Non-Compliance

1. **Late Salary Payments:** Fines and Nitaqat downgrade
2. **Missing Contracts:** Cannot process government services
3. **Low Saudization:** Cannot hire expatriates, lose government contracts
4. **No Medical Insurance:** Company fines
