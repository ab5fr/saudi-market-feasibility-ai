TITLE: Balady - Municipal License Requirements
AUTHORITY: Balady Platform (Ministry of Municipal and Rural Affairs)
URL: https://balady.gov.sa
DATE: 2024

## Overview
Balady is the unified platform for all municipal services in Saudi Arabia. All commercial establishments require a municipal license to operate legally.

## Types of Municipal Licenses

### 1. Commercial License (CR)
- **Required For:** All retail, food, and service businesses
- **Validity:** 1 year (renewable)
- **Fee:** SAR 2,000 - 10,000 (varies by city and activity)

### 2. Industrial License
- **Required For:** Manufacturing and industrial activities
- **Additional Requirements:** Environmental permits
- **Processing Time:** 30-60 days

### 3. Professional License
- **Required For:** Offices, clinics, consultancies
- **Requirements:** Professional qualifications

## Application Process

### Step 1: Location Requirements
- Lease contract attested via Ejar platform
- Space meets municipal requirements
- Building has valid completion certificate

### Step 2: Online Application
1. Register on https://balady.gov.sa
2. Submit required documents
3. Pay applicable fees
4. Schedule inspection (if required)

### Step 3: Inspection
- Municipal inspector visits location
- Verifies compliance with regulations
- May require modifications

### Step 4: License Issuance
- Digital license issued
- Must display license at business location

## Required Documents

### General Requirements
- Commercial Registration (CR)
- Lease contract (attested)
- Building completion certificate
- Floor plan
- Owner/Manager ID

### Activity-Specific Requirements

#### Food & Beverage
- Food safety certificate
- Kitchen layout approval
- Waste management plan
- Staff health certificates

#### Retail
- Signage design approval
- Store layout
- Product storage specifications

#### Healthcare
- Medical license from Ministry of Health
- Staff qualifications
- Equipment specifications
- Infection control plan

## Signage Regulations

### Requirements
- Arabic text mandatory
- English text optional
- No offensive or inappropriate content
- Proper size ratios
- Approval required before installation

### Signage Permit Fees
- Small sign: SAR 500-1,000
- Medium sign: SAR 1,000-3,000
- Large sign: SAR 3,000-10,000

## Inspection Checklist

Municipal inspectors check for:
- [ ] Fire safety compliance
- [ ] Emergency exits
- [ ] Accessibility for disabled
- [ ] Sanitation facilities
- [ ] Waste disposal systems
- [ ] Proper ventilation
- [ ] Electrical safety
- [ ] Structural integrity

## Renewal Process

### Timeline
- **Renewal Window:** 60 days before expiry
- **Grace Period:** 30 days after expiry (with fines)

### Renewal Fees
- Standard renewal: Same as initial license
- Late renewal: +20% penalty
- After grace period: New application required

## Common Violations and Fines

| Violation | Fine (SAR) |
|-----------|-----------|
| Operating without license | 10,000 - 50,000 |
| Expired license | 5,000 - 10,000 |
| Unauthorized signage | 2,000 - 5,000 |
| Health/safety violations | 5,000 - 20,000 |
| Unapproved modifications | 5,000 - 15,000 |

## Special Zones

### Free Zones
- Certain industrial cities have simplified procedures
- Lower fees in some economic zones
- Special incentives for specific activities

### Heritage Areas
- Stricter design requirements
- Special signage regulations
- Limited activities allowed

## Important Notes

1. **Display Requirement:** License must be visibly displayed at entrance
2. **Modifications:** Any location changes require re-approval
3. **Activity Changes:** New license required for different activities
4. **Sub-leasing:** Generally prohibited without approval
