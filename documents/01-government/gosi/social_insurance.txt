TITLE: GOSI Registration and Social Insurance Requirements
AUTHORITY: General Organization for Social Insurance (GOSI)
URL: https://www.gosi.gov.sa
DATE: 2024

## Overview
GOSI is the Saudi government agency responsible for social insurance for private sector employees. Registration is mandatory for all employers.

## Registration Process

### Who Must Register
- All employers in the private sector
- Saudi and non-Saudi employees
- Domestic workers (separate system)

### Registration Steps
1. **Online Registration:** https://www.gosi.gov.sa
2. **Document Upload:**
   - Commercial Registration
   - Owner/Manager ID
   - Employee list with IDs
3. **Verification:** 3-5 business days
4. **GOSI Number Issued**

## Contribution Rates (2024)

### For Saudi Employees
- **Employer Contribution:** 11% of salary
- **Employee Contribution:** 11% of salary
- **Total:** 22% of monthly wage

### For Non-Saudi Employees
- **Employer Contribution:** 2% of salary (occupational hazards only)
- **Employee Contribution:** 0%
- **Total:** 2% of monthly wage

## Coverage and Benefits

### For Saudi Employees
- Retirement pension
- Disability benefits
- Death benefits
- Occupational injury compensation
- Unemployment benefits (SANED)

### For Non-Saudi Employees
- Occupational injury compensation
- Death benefits (lump sum)

## Monthly Reporting

- **Deadline:** 15th of following month
- **Method:** Online portal or approved payroll systems
- **Penalties:** Late submission fines

## Saudization Impact

GOSI registration affects:
- Nitaqat program compliance
- Government contract eligibility
- Work visa issuance

## Important Notes

- Registration must occur within 30 days of hiring first employee
- Delayed registration incurs fines
- Monthly payments are mandatory
- Employee cannot be registered with multiple employers
