TITLE: Commercial Registration (CR) Requirements and Procedures
AUTHORITY: Ministry of Commerce (MOC)
URL: https://mc.gov.sa
DATE: 2024

## Overview
Commercial Registration (CR) is the primary license required to conduct business in Saudi Arabia. It is issued by the Ministry of Commerce.

## Types of Business Entities

### 1. Limited Liability Company (LLC)
- **Minimum Capital:** No minimum required
- **Shareholders:** 2 to 50 shareholders
- **Liability:** Limited to capital contribution
- **Best For:** Most SME businesses

### 2. Sole Proprietorship
- **Owner:** Single Saudi national
- **Liability:** Unlimited
- **Best For:** Small retail, individual services

### 3. Joint Stock Company
- **Minimum Capital:** SAR 500,000
- **Shareholders:** Minimum 2 shareholders
- **Best For:** Large businesses, public offerings

## CR Application Process

### Step 1: Prepare Documents
Required documents:
- Copy of valid ID for all shareholders
- Lease contract (Ejara) attested by Ejar platform
- Municipal license (Balady) for physical locations
- Activity code selection

### Step 2: Online Application
- Portal: https://mc.gov.sa
- Processing Time: 1-3 business days
- Fee: SAR 500 (basic registration)

### Step 3: Obtain Commercial Registration
- CR Number issued
- Can commence business operations

## Associated Licenses by Activity

### Food and Beverage
- Municipal license (Balady)
- Food safety certificate (SFDA)
- Health cards for employees

### Retail
- Municipal license (Balady)
- Signage permit

### Technology/Consulting
- No additional physical license required
- May need professional certification

### Manufacturing
- Industrial license (MODON)
- Environmental permits
- Safety compliance certificates

## Renewal and Maintenance

- **CR Validity:** 1 year (renewable)
- **Renewal Fee:** SAR 500
- **Grace Period:** 3 months after expiry

## Related Registrations

After obtaining CR:
1. **Qiwa Registration:** For employee contracts
2. **GOSI Registration:** Social insurance
3. **ZATCA Registration:** Tax and Zakat
4. **Mudad Registration:** Wage protection system

## Fees Summary

- Commercial Registration: SAR 500
- Municipal License (varies by city): SAR 2,000-10,000
- Chamber of Commerce Membership: SAR 1,000-5,000/year

## Important Notes

- Foreign ownership allowed in many sectors (check MISA for exceptions)
- Saudization requirements apply based on company size
- Certain activities require special permits
